Step 5 of 6

Share your Google Calendar

Allow Prio AI to add booked meetings directly to your calendar.

ai-caller@prioai.iam.gserviceaccount.com
1

Open Google Calendar Settings

Go to calendar.google.com and click the ⚙ Settings gear icon in the top-right corner.

2

Select your calendar

In the left sidebar, under Settings for my calendars, click on the calendar you want to share (usually your primary one).

3

Find "Share with specific people"

Scroll down to the Share with specific people or groups section and click + Add people and groups.

4

Paste the service account email

Paste the Prio AI email address shown above into the email field.

Use the Copy button above to copy the email to your clipboard.

5

Set permission level

From the permissions dropdown, select Make changes to events so Prio AI can add meetings to your calendar.

6

Click Send

Click Send to share your calendar. Meetings booked by our AI will now appear automatically.

Why do I need to do this?

When our AI caller books a meeting with a lead, it automatically creates a Google Calendar event so you never miss an appointment. This requires calendar write access.
